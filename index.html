How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

1. Pick Your Species

Select from a wide range of poultry — each with accurate incubation periods baked in.

2. Set Your Date

Our Apps — Plant Health & Soil

NutriRoots

Nurturing roots. Improving yields. A complete plant health, disease diagnosis, companion planting, and fertilizer management platform.

Plant Health & Soil Science

Your Whole Garden in One App

NutriRoots is a mobile-first app that helps gardeners and growers assess root and overall plant health, diagnose diseases, plan companion planting, evaluate fertilizer options, and get step-by-step remediation — all tailored to your species, soil, climate, and growing system.

  • Root and above-ground health assessment with photo upload
  • Disease diagnosis with confidence levels and treatment plans
  • Evidence-based companion planting with spatial layouts
  • Precise fertilizer dosing — N-P-K, micronutrients, and calculators
  • Tailored to container, raised bed, or in-ground systems
  • Unified report: root issues, diseases, companions, and fertilizer plan
Ask About NutriRoots
Under the Hood

Core Capabilities

Four powerful engines working together to keep your plants thriving.

Disease Detection

Identify what's wrong — fast — and know exactly what to do about it.

  • Symptom checklist for leaf, stem, root, and fruit issues
  • Photo upload for visual diagnosis
  • Rule-based engine covering fungal, bacterial, viral, and pest problems
  • Treatment options: cultural, biological, and approved organic controls
  • Quarantine and disposal guidance to limit spread

Companion Planting

Know what to plant together — and what to keep apart.

  • Evidence-based pairings and avoidance lists
  • Functional groups: nitrogen-fixers, trap crops, pollinator attractors
  • Spatial layouts tailored to root architecture
  • Succession planting and seasonal timing guidance
  • Ready-to-use intercropping templates

Fertilizer Guidance

The right nutrients, in the right amount, at the right time.

  • Fertilizer chooser matched to crop, growth stage, and soil tests
  • N-P-K recommendations with micronutrient considerations
  • Warnings for over/under-application risks
  • Organic vs synthetic trade-off guidance
  • Calculators: mixing, grams per m², per pot, and drip/fertigation

Unified Reports

One assessment, one report — everything you need to act.

  • Combined root issues, disease findings, and companion recommendations
  • Fertilizer plan with dosages and timing
  • Prioritized actions: low-effort cultural fixes first, then targeted inputs
  • Prompts for when to seek lab confirmation or professional help
  • Regional pesticide and legal restriction warnings
Our Apps — Chronic & Wellness Care

Pet Health RX Manager

More than a health tracker — a chronic disease management companion for the animals you love.

Chronic & Wellness Care

Health Management Built for Real Life

Pet Health RX Manager goes beyond basic wellness tracking. It's designed for pet owners managing chronic conditions — epilepsy, diabetes, obesity, and more — with the tools to track medications, monitor patterns, log episodes, and keep your vet in the loop.

  • Chronic disease dashboards — seizure logs, blood glucose tracking, weight plans
  • Medication schedules with dosage tracking and refill reminders
  • Vet visit history, notes, and upcoming appointment alerts
  • Episode and symptom logging with patterns and trends over time
  • Multi-pet support — dogs, cats, and other companion animals
  • Exportable health reports to share with your veterinarian
  • Vaccination records and preventive care schedules
  • Weight and nutrition tracking for obesity management
Ask About Pet Health RX
When It's More Than a Checkup

Built for Chronic Care

When your pet has an ongoing condition, you need more than a calendar — you need a system.

Epilepsy Management

Track seizure frequency, duration, severity, and triggers. See patterns over time and share detailed logs with your vet to fine-tune treatment.

Diabetes Tracking

Log blood glucose readings, insulin doses, meals, and activity. Monitor trends to help keep levels stable between vet visits.

Obesity & Weight

Set target weights, track progress, log meals and exercise. Get a clear picture of whether the plan is working — or needs adjusting.

Medication Manager

Never miss a dose. Track multiple medications across multiple pets with schedules, dosage history, and refill alerts.
